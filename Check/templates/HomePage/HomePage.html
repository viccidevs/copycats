About Project

Need to check plagiarism in documents? You have come to the right site! We aim at providing service without the need to register. Checkout the tool in action and do leave feedback.

What Do We Do?

  • Compare two documents and check the percentage of match between them.
  • Display matched portion of the two documents.

How does it work

  • Upload the files you want to compare
  • Click on the compare button
  • Wait for a few milliseconds
  • The match and portions matching are displayed.
